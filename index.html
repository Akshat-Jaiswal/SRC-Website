SRC
SGSITS Robotics Club

It's a group of students of SGSITS, who share the common interest of developing ingenious ideas and technical potential into real things. The platform where your practical knowledge of ciruitry, expertise in coding and mechansim development ability will be nurtured, tested and implemented... Be it manual, autonomous, wireless bots, sensors, micro-controllers,workshops or competitions...you name it and you get it!!! All you've 2 do is Just simply Transduce Your Imagination..!!
